# EX-02-Cross-Platform-Prompting-Evaluating-Diverse-Techniques-in-AI-Powered-Text-Summarization

## AIM
To evaluate and compare the effectiveness of prompting techniques (zero-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought, role-based) across different AI platforms (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Copilot) in a specific task: text summarization.

## Scenario:
You are part of a content curation team for an educational platform that delivers quick summaries of research papers to undergraduate students. Your task is to summarize a 500-word technical article on "The Basics of Blockchain Technology" using multiple AI platforms and prompting strategies.

Your goal is to determine which combination of prompting technique + platform provides the best summary in terms of:

Accuracy

Coherence

Simplicity

Speed

User experience

# Scenario:

An educational content platform wants to provide quick and easy-to-understand summaries of technical research papers for undergraduate students. The system uses different AI platforms and prompting techniques to summarize a 500-word article on **“The Basics of Blockchain Technology.”**
The article explains blockchain concepts such as decentralized ledgers, blocks, cryptographic hashing, transparency, security, and applications in cryptocurrencies and supply chain management.

---

# 1. Straightforward Prompt

# Prompt:

```text
"Summarize the following technical article on 'The Basics of Blockchain Technology' in simple language suitable for undergraduate students."
```

# Outputs:

# ChatGPT:

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that stores data in blocks connected in chronological order. Each block contains transaction data and a cryptographic hash that links it to the previous block, making the system secure and difficult to alter. Blockchain removes the need for a central authority and is widely used in cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, smart contracts, and supply chain tracking.

# Claude:

Blockchain technology is a secure and transparent method of recording transactions across multiple computers. Data is stored in linked blocks that cannot easily be changed, ensuring trust and security. It is commonly applied in cryptocurrency systems, digital contracts, and secure data sharing environments.

# Gemini:

Blockchain is a distributed database where information is stored in connected blocks protected using cryptography. Since all participants share the same ledger, the system is transparent and resistant to tampering. Blockchain technology supports cryptocurrencies, digital identity systems, and logistics tracking.

# Copilot:

Blockchain is a digital ledger technology that records transactions in secure blocks connected together. Because the ledger is shared across many computers, it is decentralized and highly secure. Blockchain is mainly used in cryptocurrency, banking, healthcare, and supply chain applications.

# Evaluation:

| Criteria               | ChatGPT            | Claude         | Gemini        | Copilot    |
| ---------------------- | ------------------ | -------------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| **Relevance**          | ✅ High             | ✅ High         | ✅ High        | ✅ High     |
| **Coherence**          | ✅ Natural          | ✅ Natural      | ✅ Clear       | ✅ Clear    |
| **Technical Accuracy** | ✅ Strong           | ✅ Strong       | ✅ Strong      | ✅ Moderate |
| **Responsiveness**     | ✅ Fast             | ✅ Fast         | ✅ Fast        | ✅ Fast     |
| **Tone**               | ✅ Student-Friendly | ✅ Professional | ✅ Informative | ✅ Simple   |

**Winner:** ChatGPT – Most balanced combination of clarity, technical detail, and readability.

# 2. Tabular Format Prompting

# Prompt:

```text

Concept	                Description
Blockchain	            Decentralized digital ledger
Blocks	                Store transaction data
Hashing	                Provides security
Decentralization	    No central authority
Applications	        Cryptocurrency, supply chain
```

# Outputs:

# ChatGPT:

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that securely stores transactions in connected blocks. Cryptographic hashing protects data integrity, while decentralization removes dependence on central authorities. Blockchain is widely used in cryptocurrencies and supply chain management.

# Claude:

Blockchain technology uses connected blocks to record and secure transaction data through cryptographic hashing. Since the ledger is decentralized, no single authority controls the system. Applications include cryptocurrency networks and transparent supply chains.

# Gemini:

Blockchain is a secure decentralized system where transactions are stored in linked blocks. Hashing ensures security and prevents unauthorized changes. It is commonly applied in Bitcoin, digital payments, and logistics tracking.

# Copilot:

Blockchain stores information in secure digital blocks connected together. The system works without central control and uses hashing for protection. Popular uses include cryptocurrency and business transaction management.

# Evaluation:

| Criteria               | ChatGPT        | Claude         | Gemini        | Copilot    |
| ---------------------- | -------------- | -------------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| **Relevance**          | ✅ High         | ✅ High         | ✅ High        | ✅ Moderate |
| **Coherence**          | ✅ Structured   | ✅ Structured   | ✅ Structured  | ✅ Simple   |
| **Technical Accuracy** | ✅ Strong       | ✅ Strong       | ✅ Strong      | ✅ Moderate |
| **Responsiveness**     | ✅ Fast         | ✅ Fast         | ✅ Fast        | ✅ Fast     |
| **Tone**               | ✅ Professional | ✅ Professional | ✅ Informative | ✅ Basic    |

# Result:

Thus, the experiment compared four AI platforms — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot — using three prompting techniques: Straightforward Prompting, Tabular Format Prompting, and Missing Word Prompting for summarizing a technical article on **“The Basics of Blockchain Technology.”**
